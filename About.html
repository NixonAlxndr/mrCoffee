    Our History

    It all started with a simple dream — to create a space where people could slow down, reconnect, and enjoy the warmth of a truly good cup of coffee. In the fast-paced rhythm of city life, we felt there was a need for a comforting pause, a moment to breathe, and a place that felt like home. Born in a quiet corner of the city in 2015, our coffee shop began as a small, family-run café fueled not by capital, but by passion. We had no elaborate marketing, no grand strategy — just a deep love for the craft of coffee-making and the hope that others would feel it too. Every inch of our first shop was built by hand, and every cup we served was brewed with intention and heart. It wasn’t about volume or speed; it was about care. And that principle remains at our core.

    From the very beginning, we believed that where the coffee comes from matters just as much as how it’s brewed. That’s why we sought out relationships with ethical, sustainable farms around the world — from the highlands of Ethiopia to the rainforests of Colombia. Each bean is selected not only for its unique flavor profile but for the integrity of the people who cultivate it. We worked closely with farmers and roasters who shared our commitment to quality and environmental care. These partnerships allowed us to tell richer stories through our brews, ones that span continents and cultures. Over the years, the shop evolved into something more than just a place to grab a drink. It became a haven where friendships blossomed, ideas were exchanged, and communities formed — all centered around the humble ritual of sharing a cup of coffee.

    Meet the Founder –
    A Cup of Vision

    mrCoffee was born from the dream of an entrepreneurial barista named Jhon. Starting from a small shop in Jakarta that could only seat 8 people, Daniel started his business with one simple goal — to change the way people enjoy coffee.


    Inspired by his travels around the world and meetings with local coffee farmers, Jhon designed mrCoffee as a place that not only sells coffee, but also presents the story behind every cup. With a passion for sustainability and dedication to quality, he led his team to build a brand that is now known and trusted globally.
